Ingredients:
 - 100g sugar
 - 200g butter
 - 300g flour
 - 1 egg yolk
 - apples


Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees C. Grease a round baking pans with butter.
 
 2. In a large bowl, combine the sugar, flour, butter (cold) and egg yolk.
    Mix quickly until smooth. nto fridge for 30 min 
 
 3. Cut apples ino pieces

 4. Roll out dough into the baking pan, and cover with apples.

 5. Bake in oven for 30 minutes
